About Curriott Hill Residential Park

Curriott Hill Residential Park is designed as a low-density, high-quality living space for those over 55. Our vision is to create a friendly, welcoming community where residents can enjoy a peaceful retirement.

Benefits of Our Community

  • Spacious, well-designed park homes
  • Beautiful countryside setting
  • Low-maintenance, hassle-free living
  • Close to local amenities

Frequently Asked Questions

Will this development increase traffic?

No, our low-density community means minimal impact on local roads.

How will local infrastructure be affected?

We are working closely with authorities to ensure all necessary services are in place.
